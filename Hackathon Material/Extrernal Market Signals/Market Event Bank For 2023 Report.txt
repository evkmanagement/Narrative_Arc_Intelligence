## SIG-POL-2022-001

**Date**  
2022-08-16

**Headline**  
Inflation Reduction Act overhauls federal EV tax credit and adds North America final assembly requirement

**Category**  
Policy

**Signal Class**  
Structural

**Signal Vector**  
Policy

**Signal Direction**  
Policy Change

**Effective Window**  
- Start: 2022-08-16 (IRA signed; new final assembly rule begins for purchases after this date)  
- End: 2032-12-31 (clean vehicle credit authorization period under IRA)[web:229][web:232][web:370]

**Observed Change**  
On August 16, 2022, the Inflation Reduction Act was signed into law, renaming the existing plug‑in vehicle credit as the “clean vehicle credit,” extending it through 2032, eliminating the 200,000‑vehicle manufacturer cap, and introducing new income and MSRP caps and battery/critical mineral sourcing requirements that phase in starting in 2023.[web:232][web:370] Effective for vehicles purchased after August 16, 2022 and before year‑end 2022, the Act required final assembly in North America for a vehicle to qualify for the credit, while other pre‑existing rules remained in effect for that period.[web:229][web:353]

**Evidence Themes**  
Policy, Incentives, Affordability, Eligibility Rules, Final Assembly Location, Battery Sourcing

**Primary Source**  
U.S. Treasury/IRS FAQ on Inflation Reduction Act EV provisions and law firm summaries of IRA’s EV credit changes[web:229][web:232][web:370]

**Evidence Strength**  
High — federal guidance and detailed legal analyses of enacted legislation[web:229][web:232]

**Verification Status**  
Verified
